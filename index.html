Delhi Public School Harni


About Us

About twelve years ago, Sanskriti Educational Society was founded by a group of like-minded individuals with the aim of providing a school which would offer world class education to the children of Vadodara. Towards this objective, the Society entered into an agreement with the Delhi Public School Society, which is known worldwide for its 200+ schools rendering quality education to lakhs of students. Thus was born the first Delhi Public School at Vadodara.In the short span of time the school, popularly known as DPS Vadodara, has performed beyond all expectations. With more than three thousand students on its rolls, DPS Vadodara is fulfilling the dreams of its students and their parents as well. It is joy always to witness our students excelling in all spheres - academic, co-curricular and sports. But even before the first academic year rolled by, we realised that just one DPS was not enough for Vadodara. Parents from all walks of life inundated us with enquiries regarding admission of their wards - we had to perforce say 'no' to them, for the simple reason that we would not have been able to do justice to all our students in the existing infrastructure. The demand sought another good school in the city and that led to the establishment of Delhi Public School Harni. DPS Harni now caters to students from classes Pre-Nursery to XII. Delhi Public School Harni serves with full-fledged faculty and its own infrastructure. The School is easily accessible to people living in areas like Kareli Bagh, Sama, Fatehganj, Nizampura, Harni, Chhani, GSFC Township, Air Force Township, Warasia Ring Road and parts of Old City area.

Campus Life

The School Campus include Class Rooms with smart boards, Playgrounds, Transport, Library, Laboratories, IT Labs, Infirmary and many more. Our class rooms are designed in a way to provide utmost comfort to our students. They are airy and well lit and also well equipped with smart boards, which are connected to our R and D Centre. Our state of the art Mathematics, Social Science, Physics, Chemistry and Biology laboratories provide opportunity and environment to all children to learn through self-exploration and hands-on experimentation. LCD monitors are installed in all the computer labs, library and administrative block. The campus is Wi-Fi enabled. The school infirmary is well equipped to provide first aid and primary medical care in case of emergencies. A fulltime in house nurse is employed to attend to the children. The school has its own fleet of comfortable buses designed as per standards and manned by trained drivers and personnel sensitized to the needs of small children.
